Pairing Rules

DO

  • Be respectful
  • Talk to one another about the work
  • Explain what you are doing
  • Think ahead and make suggestions
  • Switch roles often

DO NOT

  • Be a bossy navigator
  • Grab the driver's mouse/keyboard/tablet
