Code.org Stages 3 & 4

Pair Programming Rules

  • DO: Be respectful
  • DO: Talk to one another about the work.
  • DO: Explain what you are doing
  • DO: Think ahead and make suggestions
  • DO: Switch roles often.
  • DON'T: Be a bossy navigator.
  • DON'T: Grab the driver's mouse/keyboard/tablet

And have fun!
